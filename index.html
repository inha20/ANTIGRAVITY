What This Repository Is

ANTIGRAVITY is the operational layer of the Structure Recognition Research Program. It does not contain research papers. It contains the infrastructure that makes long-term AI-assisted research possible: handover documents, session logs, architecture decisions, and workflow guidelines.

Operating Principles

  • 1Preserve all existing filenames unless explicitly asked to rename.
  • 2Preserve discovery history — never delete historical material.
  • 3Prefer incremental improvements over full rewrites.
  • 4Document all significant decisions in CHANGELOG or handover files.
  • 5Avoid forcing theoretical frameworks onto early observations.
  • 6Prefer conceptual organization over producing more content.
  • 7When in doubt, preserve more information rather than less.
  • 8Image rule: All figures must use human-created style — Python/LaTeX/SVG code, or researcher-created tools. AI image generation services are prohibited.
